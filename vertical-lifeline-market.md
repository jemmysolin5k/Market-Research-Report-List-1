<p><ul><li>Rigid Vertical Lifeline</li><li>Flexible Vertical Lifeline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vertical lifeline market is primarily categorized into two types: rigid and flexible vertical lifelines. Rigid vertical lifelines consist of fixed, strong materials that provide a stable safety anchor for workers at height, minimizing sway and ensuring reliable support during operations. In contrast, flexible vertical lifelines are made of adaptable materials, allowing for greater movement and flexibility while still ensuring safety. Both types serve crucial roles in fall protection systems, catering to different needs based on the working environment.</p></p>
