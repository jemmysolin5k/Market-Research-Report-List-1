<p><ul><li>Injection Liquid Type</li><li>Cryopump of Closed Cycle Gas Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum cryo pumps market comprises two main types: the injection liquid type and the closed cycle gas type. Injection liquid cryo pumps utilize cryogenic liquids, like liquid nitrogen or helium, to effectively condense and remove gas molecules from a vacuum chamber. In contrast, closed cycle gas cryo pumps use a closed-loop refrigeration system to achieve similar results, maintaining low temperatures without the need for continuous liquid supply. Both types are essential for achieving ultra-high vacuum conditions in various industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
