<p><strong>The Container Gantry Cranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Main Girder</li><li>Double Main Girder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The container gantry cranes market is divided into two main types: single main girder and double main girder cranes. Single main girder cranes are characterized by a single beam, offering a lighter, more economical option suitable for smaller operations with less demanding lifting capacities. In contrast, double main girder cranes feature two parallel beams, providing increased strength and stability, making them ideal for larger ports and heavy-duty applications where higher lifting capacities and greater operational efficiency are required.</p></p>
