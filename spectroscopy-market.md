<p><p>The spectroscopy market encompasses various types, including mass spectroscopy, molecular spectroscopy, and atomic spectroscopy. Mass spectroscopy is utilized for identifying and quantifying substances based on mass-to-charge ratios, making it essential in fields like proteomics and drug development. Molecular spectroscopy focuses on the interaction of light with molecular bonds, aiding in chemical analysis and environmental monitoring. Atomic spectroscopy, on the other hand, examines the interaction of light with atoms, primarily for elemental analysis in industries like metallurgy and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
