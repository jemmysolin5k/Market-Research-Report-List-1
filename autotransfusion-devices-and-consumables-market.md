<p><p>The Autotransfusion Devices and Consumables market encompasses various types, including on-pump and off-pump transfusion devices. On-pump transfusion devices operate during surgical procedures involving a cardiopulmonary bypass, collecting and reinfusing a patient’s blood. Off-pump devices, however, are used without a bypass system, allowing blood collection during non-cardiac surgeries or trauma cases. In addition to these devices, the consumables market includes items such as collection bags and filters essential for blood processing and storage, ensuring safe and efficient transfusions.</p></p>
