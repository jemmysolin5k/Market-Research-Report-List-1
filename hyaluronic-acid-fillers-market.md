<p><p>The hyaluronic acid fillers market is primarily divided into two types: single-phase and duplex fillers. Single-phase fillers have a uniform gel consistency, offering smooth injection and results for subtle enhancements. They are ideal for delicate areas like lips and under the eyes. Duplex fillers, on the other hand, feature a dual-consistency composition, combining a thicker central gel for volume with a softer outer layer for a natural appearance. This design allows for more significant corrections while maintaining a smooth, natural look.</p></p>
