<p><p>The Automotive Active Rear Axle Steering market is segmented into two main types: Single Actuator and Dual Actuator systems. Single Actuator systems utilize one actuator to control the rear wheels, providing enhanced maneuverability and stability during low-speed and high-speed driving. In contrast, Dual Actuator systems employ two actuators, allowing for more precise and responsive steering adjustments, delivering superior handling and cornering performance. Both technologies aim to improve vehicle dynamics and driver experience while enhancing safety and control.</p></p>
