<p><p>The high-pressure hydrogen cylinder market is categorized into four types based on their construction. Type I cylinders are made of metal and are the heaviest, suited for high-pressure applications. Type II cylinders are metallic but include a composite overwrap for weight reduction. Type III cylinders use a composite material and are lighter than Type II but still have a metallic liner. Type IV cylinders are fully composite, offering the lightest option and best resistance to environmental factors, making them ideal for various applications.</p></p>
