<p><strong>The Colonoscopy Bowel Preparation Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isosmotic Agents</li><li>Hyposmotic Agents</li><li>Hyperosmotic Agents</li><li>Combination Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Colonoscopy bowel preparation drugs are categorized into four main types: isosmotic agents, hyposmotic agents, hyperosmotic agents, and combination agents. Isosmotic agents maintain fluid balance, minimizing electrolyte disturbances. Hyposmotic agents draw water into the bowel, promoting softer stools, while hyperosmotic agents increase osmotic pressure, facilitating bowel evacuation. Combination agents utilize various mechanisms for effective cleansing. Each type aims to optimize bowel preparation for colonoscopy, ensuring patient safety and procedure efficacy by providing clear visualization of the colon.</p></p>
