<p><p>The semiconductor vacuum pump market features various types, each catering to specific applications. Dry pumps operate without fluid, ensuring clean environments. Multi-stage Roots pumps offer high efficiency and fast evacuation rates, making them suitable for larger systems. Turbomolecular pumps achieve ultra-high vacuum levels, ideal for delicate semiconductor fabrication processes. Claw pumps provide reliable and maintenance-free operation, advantageous for continuous processes. Together, these pumps support the semiconductor industry's stringent vacuum requirements, enhancing production efficiency and quality.</p></p>
