<p><p>The square hollow section (SHS) steel market is primarily divided into two types: welded SHS and seamless SHS. Welded SHS is produced by welding flat steel plates into a hollow square shape, making it cost-effective and suitable for various structural applications. Seamless SHS, on the other hand, is manufactured without any joints, providing superior strength and integrity, making it ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, manufacturing, and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
