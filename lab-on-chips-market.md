<p><p>The Lab-on-Chip (LoC) market encompasses various types, including CD-ROM Chips and EFAD Chips. CD-ROM Chips integrate multiple laboratory functions onto a compact chip, allowing efficient biochemical analysis and diagnostics. In contrast, EFAD Chips, or Electronic Fluidic Automated Devices, focus on automating fluid handling processes, enhancing reproducibility and accuracy in experiments. Both types aim to streamline laboratory workflows, reduce costs, and improve access to advanced analytical techniques across diverse fields, including healthcare, environmental monitoring, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
