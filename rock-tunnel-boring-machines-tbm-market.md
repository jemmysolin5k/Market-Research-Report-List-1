<p><p>Rock Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) are advanced mechanisms used for excavating tunnels in various geological conditions. The open-type TBM is suitable for soft ground, allowing direct access to the excavation face, ideal for shallow tunnels. In contrast, shielded hard rock TBMs are designed for challenging environments, using a protective shield to stabilize the tunnel while boring through hard rock. Both types are essential for infrastructure projects, providing efficient and safe tunneling solutions in different terrains.</p></p>
