<p><ul><li>E-ink E-Reader</li><li>TFT-LCD E-reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The e-reader market is primarily divided into two types: E-ink and TFT-LCD e-readers. E-ink devices utilize electronic paper technology, providing a reading experience similar to printed pages, with low power consumption and excellent visibility in sunlight, making them ideal for reading books. In contrast, TFT-LCD e-readers feature vibrant displays suitable for multimedia content, offering color and faster refresh rates, which can enhance user engagement but may lead to eye strain during prolonged reading sessions. Each type caters to distinct user preferences.</p></p>
