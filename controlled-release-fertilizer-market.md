<p><p>The controlled-release fertilizer market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Organic Polymer Coated**: These fertilizers utilize natural polymers to create a barrier that regulates nutrient release, promoting long-term availability and minimizing environmental impact.</p><p>2. **Inorganic Sulfur**: Derived from sulfur-based compounds, these fertilizers release nutrients slowly through microbial activity, enhancing soil fertility while reducing leaching.</p><p>3. **Combined Inorganic and Organic**: This hybrid approach integrates both inorganic and organic components, benefiting from the quick nutrient availability of inorganic fertilizers alongside the sustained release and soil health advantages of organic materials.</p></p>
