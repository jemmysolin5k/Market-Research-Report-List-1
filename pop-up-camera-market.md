<p><p>The pop-up camera market includes three main types: motorized pop-up cameras, manual slide-out cameras, and spring-loaded pop-up cameras. Manual slide-out cameras require user intervention to extend, offering simplicity and lower costs. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and technological integration in smartphones and other devices.</p></p>
