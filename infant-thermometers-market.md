<p><p>The infant thermometers market comprises three main types: digital, ear, and forehead thermometers. Digital thermometers provide quick and accurate temperature readings, often suitable for oral, rectal, or underarm use. Ear thermometers utilize infrared technology to read the temperature inside the ear, offering fast results ideal for infants. Forehead thermometers, or temporal artery thermometers, detect temperature by scanning the skin surface on the forehead, providing a non-invasive and convenient option for parents. Each type caters to varying needs and preferences.</p></p>
