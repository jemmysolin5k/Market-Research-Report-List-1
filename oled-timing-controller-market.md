<p><p>The OLED Timing Controller (TCON) market is mainly categorized into two types: Independent Type and Integration Type. The Independent Type TCON operates separately from the display driver IC, providing flexibility and easy upgrades without altering the entire display system. In contrast, the Integration Type TCON combines the timing controller functions with other components like display drivers, leading to reduced design complexity and space-saving advantages. Both types cater to varying manufacturers' needs based on performance, cost, and design preferences.</p></p>
