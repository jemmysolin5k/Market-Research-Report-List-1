<p><p>The industrial ventilation equipment market encompasses various types, primarily including centrifugal fans and axial fans, along with other specialized equipment. Centrifugal fans generate airflow through rotational motion, efficiently moving air in complex systems, making them ideal for applications requiring high pressure. Axial fans, on the other hand, move air parallel to the fan's axis, suitable for applications needing large volumes of airflow at lower pressures. Other equipment may include blowers, scrubbers, and exhaust systems, addressing specific ventilation needs in diverse industries.</p></p>
