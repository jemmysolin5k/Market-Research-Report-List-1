<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Controllers</li><li>Shunt Controllers</li><li>Shunt-Series Controllers</li><li>Series-Series Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) controllers enhance the performance of power transmission networks. </p><p>- **Series Controllers** improve voltage stability and optimize power flow by inserting impedance in series with the line. </p><p>- **Shunt Controllers** provide reactive power compensation and help regulate voltage at the bus level. </p><p>- **Shunt-Series Controllers** combine both functionalities, offering flexibility in managing reactive power and enhancing overall system stability. </p><p>- **Series-Series Controllers** facilitate power flow control and voltage regulation across multiple lines, enhancing system reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
