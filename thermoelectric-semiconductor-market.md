<p><p>The thermoelectric semiconductor market comprises two main types: single-stage modules and multistage modules. Single-stage modules are designed for applications requiring moderate temperature differences and are compact, making them suitable for small-scale cooling and power generation. In contrast, multistage modules are engineered for higher efficiency in temperature differentials, often used in demanding applications such as deep cooling, refrigeration, and advanced waste heat recovery systems. Each type caters to specific thermal management needs and energy efficiency goals.</p></p>
