<p><p>The gelatin empty capsule market is primarily divided into two types: hard gelatin capsules and soft gelatin capsules. Hard gelatin capsules are commonly used for solid medications and feature two distinct parts that can be easily filled. They provide stability and a longer shelf life. In contrast, soft gelatin capsules are often used for liquid or semi-solid formulations, offering a seamless and tamper-resistant option. Both types serve as effective delivery systems for pharmaceuticals, enhancing bioavailability and ease of consumption.</p></p>
