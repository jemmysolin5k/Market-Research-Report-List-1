<p><p>The Oil and Gas Conductor Pipe market comprises several types of pipes based on their manufacturing processes. Electric Resistance Welding (ERW) pipes are produced through electric resistance techniques, making them suitable for low to medium pressure applications. Spiral Submerged Arc Welded (SSAW) pipes are characterized by their spiral weld, providing strength and flexibility, ideal for large diameter applications. Longitudinally Submerged Arc Welding (LSAW) pipes utilize a longitudinal welding process for enhanced structural integrity. Other types may include seamless pipes and specialty materials tailored for specific needs in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
