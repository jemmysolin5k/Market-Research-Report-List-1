<p><p>The food grade coated paper market consists of three types: Type I, Type II, and Type III. Type I is primarily used for direct food contact, featuring high moisture resistance and excellent barrier properties. Type II offers good printability and moderate barrier properties, suitable for packaging that requires some protection but not direct contact. Type III focuses on cost-effectiveness, often used for secondary packaging where direct food contact is not an issue, prioritizing functionality over barrier performance.</p></p>
