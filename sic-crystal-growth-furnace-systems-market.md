<p><p>The SiC crystal growth furnace systems market primarily includes induction heating furnaces and resistance heating furnaces. Induction heating furnaces utilize electromagnetic fields to generate heat, offering precise temperature control and energy efficiency. In contrast, resistance heating furnaces rely on electrical resistance to produce heat, providing simplicity and ease of operation. Both systems are critical for fabricating silicon carbide crystals, widely used in power electronics and optoelectronics, contributing to advancements in high-performance semiconductor applications.</p></p>
