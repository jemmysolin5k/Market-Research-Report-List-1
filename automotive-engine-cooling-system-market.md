<p><p>The automotive engine cooling system market includes several key components designed to regulate engine temperature. The cooling fan module circulates air through the radiator to dissipate heat. Water pumps are essential for circulating coolant throughout the engine and radiator. Radiators transfer excess engine heat to the air, ensuring optimal operating temperatures. Cooling fluids, commonly known as coolants, prevent freezing and boiling while enhancing heat transfer. Together, these components maintain engine performance and longevity by preventing overheating.</p></p>
