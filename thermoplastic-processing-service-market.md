<p><p>Thermoplastic Processing Services encompass various methods for shaping and forming thermoplastic materials. Blow molding service is utilized for creating hollow parts by inflating heated plastic in a mold. Plastic extrusion service involves forcing melted plastic through a die to create continuous shapes, such as pipes and sheets. Compression molding service involves placing plastic in a heated mold, which is then compressed to form solid parts. Foam molding service produces lightweight, insulated parts by expanding thermoplastic into foam. Other market services may include techniques like injection molding and rotational molding.</p></p>
