<p><p>The medical PVC plastic market is categorized into two main types: with filler and without filler. PVC with filler incorporates additives to enhance properties like strength, durability, or flexibility, making it suitable for various medical applications such as tubing and containers. In contrast, PVC without filler maintains pure composition, offering biocompatibility and clarity, which are essential for medical devices like catheters and IV bags. The choice between these types depends on specific application requirements and regulatory standards in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
